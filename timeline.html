History of Ooo

  • Before the universe existed there were monsters. The Lich, Orgalorg, and Hunson Abadeer existed.
  • During the Cretaceous period, an Elemental named Urgence Evergreen attempted to stop a green Catalyst Comet (the Lich) from hitting Earth, but the other Elementals, Chatsberry, Balthus, and Slimy D, opposed it. The Ice Crown is created in an attempt to stop the comet from hitting Earth. Because of how Gunter uses the crown, it causes its wearer to become like Evergreen; or rather, how Gunter saw Evergreen.
  • Orgalorg the warlord was banished by Grob Gob Glob Grod to Earth for attempting to intercept a yellow Catalyst Comet to gain its power. Orgalorg is transformed into a penguin due to the planet's gravity.
  • A blue Catalyst Comet hits Earth. It is later reborn as a butterfly and a pink lump with a gold outline.
