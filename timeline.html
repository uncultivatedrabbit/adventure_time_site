History of Ooo

  • Before the universe existed there were monsters. The Lich, Orgalorg, and Hunson Abadeer existed.
  • During the Cretaceous period, an Elemental named Urgence Evergreen attempted to stop a green Catalyst Comet (the Lich) from hitting Earth, but the other Elementals, Chatsberry, Balthus, and Slimy D, opposed it. The Ice Crown is created in an attempt to stop the comet from hitting Earth. Because of how Gunter uses the crown, it causes its wearer to become like Evergreen; or rather, how Gunter saw Evergreen.
  • Orgalorg the warlord was banished by Grob Gob Glob Grod to Earth for attempting to intercept a yellow Catalyst Comet to gain its power. Orgalorg is transformed into a penguin due to the planet's gravity.
  • A blue Catalyst Comet hits Earth. It is later reborn as a butterfly and a pink lump with a gold outline.
  • Human civilization takes over the Earth.
  • 44 years B.M.W: Simon Petrikov is born.
  • 20-40 years B.M.W: Betty Grof is born.
  • 5-10 years B.M.W: Betty Grof Meets Simon and cancels her trip to Australia.
  • 4 years B.M.W: Marceline is born. She is the daughter of the Lord of Evil, Hunson Abadeer. Marceline was seen living with her mother in a trailer, with her father seemingly absent.
  • 5 years B.M.W: Simon and Betty discover the Enchiridion.
  • 1-2 years B.M.W: Simon purchases a crown from an old dock worker in northern Scandinavia, and is influenced by its powers, slowly losing his sanity, memories, and personality, becoming the Ice King. The powers of the crown are seemingly the reason he survived the mushroom bomb. Upon wearing the crown he had said things that made his fiance, Betty Grof, run away from him.
  • < 1 years B.M.W: The Elementals saw a future vision of the Mushroom War and Patience St. Pim freezes herself to survive.
  • The Mushroom War takes place. The Mushroom Bomb kills most humans on the Earth and mutates many lifeforms. It also causes a major climate change.
  • The Lich's body arises during the explosion of the Mushroom Bomb, he begins his crusade to annihilate all life in the universe.
  • 1 year A.M.W: Simon Petrikov adopts a half-demon girl, Marceline, and raises her in the new post-apocalyptic world.
  • 15 years A.M.W: Two Bread Tom leads a tribe of surviving humans into a freighter. Marceline grows up to be a vampire hunter and protects him and his tribe. Tom and his group of humans settle on an archipelago at the southwest of the surviving continent of Ooo and build its Guardian.
  • 200 years A.M.W: Princess Bubblegum and Neddy were born, by dropping from the Mother Gum.
  • 200 years A.M.W: Princess Bubblegum creates Uncle Gumbald, Aunt Lolly, and Cousin Chicle. Gumbald later tried to gain the power and created a Dum Dum Juice to make her more docile and simple-minded. The act fails and the trio all become candy people, forgetting their previous identities, leading Bubblegum to the create the Candy Kingdom.
  • 700 years A.M.W: Shoko and her tiger go on adventures.
  • 800 years A.M.W: Magic Man's wife, Margles is abducted by Golb. Magic Man later magically creates a defense device to protect Mars from Golb. Magic Man hiked to Olympus Mons with the Margles device, but of course had feeling for it as she resembled his lost wife. She then turns into a defense system, and Magic Man tries to stop them realizing it was made from his nightmares. This leads to Magic Man turn to evil.
  • Magic Man is banished from Mars by the King of Mars and Grob Gob Glob Grod and sent to Earth/Ooo.
  • 950+ years A.M.W: Billy casts the Lich down, imprisoning him in amber in the heart of the tree in the middle of the Candy Kingdom.
  • 950+ years A.M.W: Princess Bubblegum creates the Earl of Lemongrab.
  • 985 years A.M.W: Warren Ampersand comes to the Land of Ooo to pass on his stretchy powers into a compatible breeding partner. Warren finds Joshua the Dog and infects/impregnates him, leading to the birth of Jake.
  • 985 years A.M.W: Finn Mertens is born on Hub Island to Martin Mertens and Minerva Campbell.
  • 985 years A.M.W: Martin tries to surround his home island to escape Widow, but he is mistaken for a Hider by the Guardian. He leaves Finn behind on the raft and Finn drifts to Ooo, where he is eventually found/adopted and raised by Joshua and Margaret.
  • 995 years A.M.W: Finn and Jake's parents, Joshua and Margaret, pass away.
  • 995 years A.M.W: Lady Rainicorn leaves the Crystal Dimension, breaking up with her boyfriend Lee, and moves to the Land of Ooo.
  • 995+ years A.M.W: Jake is a criminal in a gang with Tiffany Oiler, the Flying Lettuce Brothers, and Gareth. He is also in the Card Wars groups and has a girlfriend named Moniker.
  • Jake writes Mind Games under the pen name Jay T. Doggzone.
  • 997 years A.M.W: Finn and Jake begin to go all across the Land of Ooo, helping others, going on quests, destroying evil, making new friends, and much more.
  • 997 years A.M.W: Finn and Jake go to Mount Cragdor and obtain the Enchiridion.
  • The Lich is accidentally released from the amber prison. Supposedly defeated, the Lich still lives on, possessing the Snail.
  • Finn and Jake find out about Ice King's origin and begin to pity him more.
  • 998 years A.M.W: Finn and Jake meet Flame Princess, who later becomes Finn's girlfriend.
  • Finn gives away the Enchiridion to a bear, who unbeknownst to him, is working for the Lich-possessed snail.
  • Grob Gob Glob Grod comes to Earth looking for Magic Man, planning to put him on trial for his past offenses on Mars.
  • The Lich leaves the snail, possesses and later kills Billy. The Lich tricks Finn into using the Enchiridion to create a portal to the multiverse. The Lich finds Prismo and wishes for the destruction of all life, but Finn and Jake stop the attempt. Finn and Jake meet Prismo, and Finn's wish leads to the creation of the Farmworld timeline.
  • The Ice King's curse is temporarily broken and he is reverted back to Simon. He creates a time portal and accidentally brings his former fiance Betty to the future. Simon is shorty reverted back to the Ice King, but Betty vows to find a cure.
  • Finn finds out about his biological father Martin and goes with Jake to the Citadel to meet him.
  • The Lich is destroyed and becomes a giant baby known as Sweet P after he is covered with the citadel guardian blood. Finn loses his arm trying to get closer to his father at the citadel.
  • By mistake, Magic Man loses his magic powers along with his insanity, and they are transferred to Betty.
  • 1000 years A.M.W: The Candy Kingdom has its very first election. Princess Bubblegum is outvoted and her opponent, the King of Ooo, becomes the new ruler of the Candy Kingdom.
  • Orgalorg emerges from Gunter and attempts to swallow the current purple Catalyst Comet to gain its powers, but is defeated. Martin accepts its offer and disappears from the universe.
  • Princess Bubblegum cures Marceline's vampirism, but in the process, accidentally revives the five vampires she gained her powers and vampirism from. In the end, the vampires are defeated and Marceline becomes a vampire once more.
  • The Candy Kingdom overthrows the King of Ooo and gives the throne back to Princess Bubblegum.
  • Prismo sends Finn and Jake to the Farmworld to stop the Farmworld Ice Finn and the Farmworld Lich/Jake from creating a portal into the multiverse with their timeline's Enchiridion. Finn and Jake cure both of their counterparts, and Finn secretly takes the Enchiridion back with him.
  • Magic Man, turned Normal Man, leaves Earth and returns to Mars, taking the place of Grob Gob Glob Grod and Abraham Lincoln as King of Mars.
  • Patience St. Pim emerges from her 1000 year hyper-sleep, as she froze herself to survive the mushroom war. She is obsessed on unleashing the full magical potential of herself and the other elementals.
  • Finn, Jake and Susan Strong leave Ooo to visit the islands, where they discover the fate of the humans.
  • Finn and Jake come home from the islands, only find out that Patience St. Pim had set up a mega-elemental spell, in which all of Ooo was possessed by the elements. The act is countered by the anti-elemental lumps of Lumpy Space Princess, reverting everyone back to their true shape. At this same time, Betty had kidnapped Ice King using the new farmworld Enchiridion to change time and prevent Simon from wearing the Ice Crown. However, Ice King interferes and Betty is teleported to Mars.
  • Due to the anti-elemental lumps of Lumpy Space Princess, Gumbald, Lolly, and Chicle are reverted back to their original selves and immediately resume usurping Princess Bubblegum.
  • 1001 years A.M.W: The hand of the Lich-possessed Farmworld Jake, which had fallen into the multiverse portal, taunts Sweet P, wanting the two to reign together. Sweet P, however, does not approve of this and destroys the hand.
  • Due to time slips set off by Clock Bear, Jake sees his own birth. Not much later, he is abducted by Warren Ampersand and taken to his supposed homeworld. Jake finds out the source of his stretchy powers and Warren's motive, which was to kill him and steal his powers. Jake throws his father into a black hole.
  • Finn and Jermaine travel to Mars to seek guidance from Normal Man turned King Man, looking for Jake. They come across Betty and the three enter a Mars temple to undergo mind challenges. Betty was supposed to be cured of her obsession with curing the Ice King but instead comes up with a plan to save Simon and King Man's wife Margles from Golb. In the process, Jake is retrieved and goes home with Finn.
  • The Candy Kingdom and Gumbaldia declare war on each other beginning the Gum War.
  • Betty and King Man use Maja to summon Golb to Ooo.
  • Finn, Betty, and Ice King are consumed by Golb. Inside Golb, Ice King becomes Simon Petrikov again, and Betty loses her magic powers. The Ice Crown is also reset to its essential form and will grant its wearer their deepest wish. Betty intended to wish Golb away from the world but instead, she wishes to protect Simon. Betty is then merged with Golb into one being.
  • Fern disintegrates and what remained of him was a small seedling in the shape of the Finn Sword. Finn and Jake plant the Fern seedling at what remained of the Tree Fort. It immediately grew into a new tree resembling the tree fort, with a new Finn Sword growing out of it.
  • The humans of the islands come to the Land of Ooo after nearly 1000 years of near-absence.
